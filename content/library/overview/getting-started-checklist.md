This checklist serves as a high-level guide for conducting a GitHub Well-Architected Assessment. It outlines the key areas to review and evaluate to ensure your organization's GitHub usage aligns with the best practices and principles of the GitHub Well-Architected Framework.

### Checklist Items

1. **Engage with an Expert:** Start by engaging with a GitHub or Partner expert to facilitate the assessment.
1. **Review the GitHub Well-Architected Framework:** Familiarize yourself with the five pillars of the framework: Productivity, Collaboration, Application Security, Governance, and Architecture.
1. **Initial GitHub Environment Review:** Conduct an initial review of your organization's GitHub environment focusing on the five key pillars.
1. **Stakeholder Interviews and Surveys:** Perform interviews and surveys with key stakeholders to gather insights into your organization's GitHub practices and challenges.
1. **Analysis and Scoring:** Analyze your organization's alignment with the framework's pillars and principles, scoring each area to identify strengths and weaknesses.
1. **Recommendation Review:** Review the recommendations provided by the expert, tailored to your organization's specific needs and goals.
